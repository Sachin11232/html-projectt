Academic Focus:

BCA's academic focus centers on computer applications and software development. Its curriculum encompasses programming languages, database management systems, web development, and software engineering. Practical learning through projects and internships is emphasized, preparing students for careers as software developers, system analysts, and other roles in the IT industry

Duration:

BCA typically spans three years, although duration may vary. This undergraduate program immerses students in computer applications and software development. The curriculum covers programming languages, database management, web development, and more, preparing graduates for careers in the IT industry or further education in related fields.

Industry Demand:

The IT industry has a high demand for BCA graduates due to their expertise in computer applications and software development. With skills in programming, database management, and web development, BCA graduates are sought after for roles such as software developer, system analyst, database administrator, and IT consultant in various industries.
